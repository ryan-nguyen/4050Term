Welcome to STEPS!

About Us

We provide a platform for any kind of sport and sports organization to plan their tournaments, provide information to fans, manage registeration of teams, players and coaches and to execute a sport league with ease

Our Facilities

1

Generalized
STEPS is not designed for any specific software but instead can be used for any sport.

2

Plan tournaments with ease
STEPS make it easier to plan events as various functions are handled by the software itself.

3

Acts as gallery for fans
STEPS also acts as a information resource for fans to know about their favourite teams and players.

4

Automatic Brackets Generation
Creates brackets for playoffs and tournaments automatically based on the teams that registered for the tournaments.

5

Plan events
Apart from tournaments, STEPS can also be used to plan events and ceremonies related to sports.
